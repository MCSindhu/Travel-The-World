WAYANAD

This popular town is famous for wildlife and its spice plantations. Wayanad is part of a forest reserve, located on the border of Tamil Nadu and Kerala. Because of it wild green coverage it offers great scenic beauty along with the experience of seeing wildlife.



AGUMBE

Agumbe Sunset Viewpoint is one of the best places to visit in the Western Ghats. This sunset point is a must to visit. For the spectacular views of the sunset, this place is very popular among tourists. The altitude of the Agumbe Sunset View Point is 825 meters. Agumbe is situated within a distance of 90 kilometers from Shimoga, which is the nearest city from Agumbe.
